LOCHQUARRY OUTDOOR CENTRE is set in the heart of the Argyll hills, surrounded by stunning scenery and endless opportunities for adventure. We offer exciting outdoor activities for young people aged 6–18, designed for all abilities and experience levels. From land and water challenges to high rope adventures, Lochquarry is the perfect place to explore, learn new skills, and enjoy the great outdoors.
Land based activities
Explore the great outdoors on foot with a range of exciting land-based adventures. From peaceful walks to high-energy challenges, these activities help you build confidence, teamwork, and navigation skills in the natural landscape around Lochquarry.
Rope based activities
Push your limits and take on exciting heights with our rope-based challenges. Designed to build courage and confidence, these activities let you climb, descend, and reach new heights safely with expert guidance and full equipment.
Water based activities
Take to the water and experience Lochquarry from a whole new perspective. Whether paddling, steering, or exploring, these activities are perfect for building balance, coordination, and teamwork while enjoying the thrill of the loch.
