About Us Staff
Each activity is run under the instruction of one of our highly trained staff and all safety equipment is provided. Let’s meet some of the Lochquarry staff.
Claire Jack
Centre Manager
Claire is responsible for the overall running of the centre and all its activities, keeping everything organised, safe, and running smoothly behind the scenes. She brings energy and enthusiasm to the team and helps create a positive experience for every visitor. Her favourite activity is the pole climb, where she enjoys the challenge and the sense of achievement it brings.
Robbie Elliot
Senior Instructor (Land)
Robbie oversees all land-based activities at the centre, making sure every session is safe, engaging, and well organised. He has a real passion for the outdoors and enjoys helping visitors build confidence and skills on land adventures. His favourite activity is hillwalking in the beautiful Scottish Highlands, where he can take in the scenery and explore the natural landscape.
Marion Hunter
Centre Administrator
Marion makes bookings and organises activity slots for visiting groups, ensuring every trip to Lochquarry runs smoothly from start to finish. She enjoys being the first point of contact for guests and takes pride in helping plan memorable experiences. Her favourite part of the job is making sure everyone has a great time when they visit Lochquarry.
